    About us

    Unity VR Dev is a friendly Slack community VR / AR enthusiasts and professionals, designing and developing using Unity3D. We share our experience, skills, knowledge, and relevant news about VR, AR, and Unity3d.

    Code of conduct

    We strongly encourage politeness, honesty, and professionalism. We do not tolerate flood, spam, harassment, and other nonsense. Those who violate the rules will be subject to being expelled from the community.
